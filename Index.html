Cpixel KitPvP Update!

I know a lot of you have been waiting for this update. Because KitPvP is the most popular Cpixel game so far, I decided to update it! This update adds 3 new kits that you can read more about in the changelog. It also adds the OP mode for Spleef that has said "Coming Soon" for a while now. I hope you all enjoy this update!




Update Changelog:

  • Added 3 KitPvP kits: The Fisherman, The Raider, and The Ninja.
  • Added OP mode for Spleef which gives you efficiency 100 golden shovels and snowballs.
  • Added Ninja Stars, a powerful weapon only found in the Ninja kit but will later be added as a Lucky block drop. They do the damage of a pistol but can only be used once. They have unlimited range.
  • Added the "Murderer" advancement for getting 500 kills.
  • Added /spawn (the same as /hub or /lobby)
  • Added /copycoords and /resetcopiedcoords. /copycoords copies your coordinates and displays them if you are in the chat or in a command block. /resetcopiedcoords will reset your copied coordinates and you wont see them in the chat or command block menu
  • Added /regen, a feature that allows players to regen their health if they were spawn fighting or after a game.
  • Added /hubaroo and /hubpvp. /hubaroo gives every player a knockback nemo. It is just a simple version of slaparoo that you can play in the hub. /hubpvp will give everyone armor and a sword so players can fight in the lobby instead of just punching eachother until they die.
  • Added Airhorns! A feature I just added for fun that plays the annoying horn sound at a high volume when used. It is also a drop in Lucky Islands.
  • General changes in Lucky Islands such as altering the height that the flying chickens spawn at and other stuff like that.
